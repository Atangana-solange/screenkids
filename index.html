Dear parents, welcome to ScreenKids

In today's digital world, screens are everywhere — smartphones, tablets, computers, televisions and video game consoles surround our children from a very young age. While technology offers real educational opportunities, excessive or unregulated screen time can have significant effects on children's health, sleep, concentration and social development.

ScreenKids is an easy-to-use application designed to help you track, understand and analyse how much time your children spend in front of screens each day. By collecting real data, you will be able to identify patterns, compare different devices and activities, and make informed decisions for your family.

The World Health Organization (WHO) recommends no more than 1 hour (60 minutes) per day of screen time for children aged 3–5, and balanced, quality screen use for older children. This app uses those guidelines to help you identify risk levels.
